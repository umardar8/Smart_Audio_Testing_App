# Smart Audio Testing and Hearing Aid (ATHA)

## Part of Final Year Project for completing Bachelor's of Engineering

## Awarded Final Year Design 2022 Award by Pakistan Engineering Council (PEC)

# Functionality

- Diagnose your hearing disability and hearing damage by generating audible frequencies under the range of 20Hz to 20KHz.
- Repel Insects, Mosquitoes, Flies, Rat, Lizards, and Cockroaches by generating Insect Repellent Frequencies.
- Generate Custom Frequencies for Experimental Purposes.
- Use as a Hearing Aid (Future work under development)
